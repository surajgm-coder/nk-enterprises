Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about NK Enterprises, our services, pricing, delivery, and support. If you need more help, feel free to contact us anytime.

1. What services does NK Enterprises provide?

NK Enterprises provides fabrication and manufacturing solutions including welding services, custom metal work, furniture fabrication, and product supply based on customer requirements.

2. How can I request a quotation?

You can request a quotation by visiting the Contact Us page, filling the inquiry form, or directly calling/WhatsApp us. Share your project details, quantity, and delivery location for faster estimation.

3. Do you provide custom fabrication work?

Yes. We specialize in custom fabrication based on drawings, measurements, and project requirements. We can also assist with design suggestions if needed.

4. What details should I share for an accurate quote?

  • Product type / project requirement
  • Size / measurements / drawings
  • Material preference (steel, SS, aluminum, etc.)
  • Quantity
  • Delivery location & timeline

5. Do you deliver products to other cities?

Yes, we can deliver depending on your location and order type. Delivery charges and timeline may vary based on distance and transport availability.

6. What is the usual delivery time?

Delivery time depends on the project size, customization, and workload. We will confirm the estimated completion timeline after reviewing your requirements.

7. What payment methods do you accept?

Payment options may include bank transfer, UPI, and other accepted modes. Final payment details will be shared during quotation confirmation.
